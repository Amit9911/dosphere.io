App Name: Taskprayuj

Developer: Amit kumar (Personal Developer)

Effective Date: January 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how personal data is collected, used, stored, shared, and protected when the Taskprayuj Android app is used.

Compliance is maintained with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable, and Google Play Store requirements. The role of Data Fiduciary under the DPDP Act is fulfilled. The app is intended exclusively for users aged 18 years and older. No data is knowingly collected or processed from children under 18.

Permissions and Device Access

To provide core functionality, the App requires access to specific device features. We only request permissions necessary for the features you choose to use:

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): We use this permission to send you reminders for tasks, maintain your habit streaks, and provide alerts for focus sessions (Pomodoro). You can withdraw this consent at any time via your device settings.
  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): We use this permission exclusively for the "Voice Journaling" feature. This allows the App to convert your spoken words into written text. Audio data is processed in real-time and is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties, except for the necessary conversion to text via system-level APIs, .

You can manage or revoke these permissions at any time through your Android System Settings.

App Widget (Glance)

Taskprayuj provides an App Widget feature that allows you to view your current tasks or habit streaks directly on your home screen. The widget accesses your local Room database to display this information. Data displayed on the widget is never transmitted to external servers specifically for the widget's functionality; it is processed entirely on-device to ensure privacy and speed.

Data Collected

Data provided by users:

  • Name, Email address and hashed password for authentication via Firebase Authentication.
  • Tasks, habits, and journal entries stored locally on the device.

Data collected automatically (via Firebase and Google services):

  • Device information (model, OS version, app version, language, approximate country from IP).
  • Usage events (sessions, feature interactions) via Firebase Analytics and Google Analytics.
  • Crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Firebase Installation ID for notifications and analytics.
  • Anonymized snippets from journals sent to Gemini models for AI habit suggestions.

No sensitive personal data (health, religion, ethnicity) is collected unless voluntarily included in locally stored journal entries. All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS.

How Data Is Used

  • Authentication and secure account management.
  • Core features: task management, habit tracking, journaling, AI suggestions, microlearning lessons, notifications.
  • Usage analysis and app improvement via Firebase Analytics and google analytics.
  • Crash diagnosis and resolution via Crashlytics.
  • App stability monitoring and updates via Google Play.
  • Legal compliance, fraud prevention, and security maintenance.

Only anonymized snippets are processed by AI; no identifiable data is retained after processing.

Sharing of Data

  • Shared with Google/Firebase under secure Data Processing and Security Terms.
  • Disclosed if required by law or to protect rights and safety.
  • Transferred in case of merger/acquisition with notice to users.
  • Data may be stored on servers outside India (e.g., US) with GDPR safeguards.

Storage, Retention, and Security

  • User content stored locally on device.
  • Temporary processing/logging on Firebase servers.
  • Analytics retained ~60 days (de-identified).
  • Crash data retained ~90 days.
  • Authentication data retained until account deletion.

Encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, ISO 27001 compliance. Breaches reported under DPDP Act and applicable laws.

User Rights

  • Access and deletion of data via in-app features.
  • Consent withdrawal by account deletion.
  • Grievance escalation internally with us.

Account Deletion: Account deletion via in-app delete Account button.

Analytics and Crash Reporting Controls

Firebase Analytics, google analytics and Crashlytics are enabled by default.
