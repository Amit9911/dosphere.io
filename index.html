Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Welcome to Taskprayuj. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Developer: Amit Kumar (Individual Developer), India

1. Information we collect

Personal identification information

Through Firebase Authentication, we collect your email address and name to provide secure access to your account and to synchronize your data across devices.

User-generated content

Tasks, journals, and habits you create within the app are stored locally on your device using Room Database and automatically synced to the cloud using Google Firestore.

AI interaction data

Taskprayuj uses Google Gemini AI to provide intelligent productivity logic. When you interact with AI features, anonymized snippets of your tasks or queries may be processed by the Gemini API to generate responses. We do not use your personal identity for AI training.

2. Technical and usage data

  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics and Google Analytics help us understand how users interact with the app (e.g., feature usage, session duration).
  • Crash reporting: Firebase Crashlytics collects bug reports and stack traces to improve app stability.
  • App updates: Google Play In-App Updates help ensure you are running the most secure version of the app.

3. How we use your information

  • Sync and continuity: Maintain and synchronize your productivity data across devices.
  • AI-driven features: Provide personalized insights and task logic using AI.
  • Performance and reliability: Monitor app performance and fix technical issues.
  • Service communications: Communicate important updates regarding the service.

4. Data storage and security

  • Cloud storage: Data is stored using industry-standard encryption provided by Google Firebase (Firestore).
  • Local storage: Local data is secured within the app’s private sandbox using Android’s Room persistence library.
  • Security commitment: While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
Note: Taskprayuj does not share your journals, habits, or tasks with advertisers or external parties beyond the third-party providers listed in this policy.

5. Third-party services

Taskprayuj uses the following third-party providers solely to deliver and improve the service:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics)
  • Google Gemini AI (AI logic)
  • Google Play Services

6. Your data rights

  • Access: You can view your data within the app at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated Firestore data directly within the app. If you prefer, you may request deletion by emailing 1019atd@gmail.com.
  • Export: If the app offers data export features, you may use them to download a copy of your data for your records.

7. Children’s privacy

Taskprayuj is not directed to anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app and on the Play Store listing, and the effective date will be revised accordingly. Where material changes occur, we will provide an in-app notice.
