Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Welcome to Taskprayuj. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Identification Information

Through Firebase Authentication, we collect information such as your email address and name to provide secure access to your account and sync your data across devices.

b. User-Generated Content

We store the tasks, journals, and habits you create within the app. This data is stored locally on your device using Room Database and automatically synced to the cloud using Google Firestore.

c. AI Interaction Data

Our app utilizes Google Gemini AI to provide intelligent productivity logic. When you interact with AI features, anonymized snippets of your tasks or queries may be processed by the Gemini API to generate responses. We do not use your personal identity for AI training.

2. Technical and Usage Data

  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics and Google Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. This includes anonymized data like feature usage and session duration.
  • Crash Reporting: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect bug reports and stack traces to improve app stability.
  • App Updates: We utilize Google Play In-App Updates to ensure you are always running the most secure version of the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Maintain and sync your productivity data across devices.
  • Provide personalized AI-driven insights and task logic.
  • Monitor app performance and fix technical issues.
  • Communicate important updates regarding the service.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using industry-standard encryption provided by Google Firebase (Firestore). Local data is secured within the app's private sandbox using Android's Room persistence library. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

Taskprayuj uses the following third-party providers:

  • Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics)
  • Google Gemini AI (AI Logic)
  • Google Play Services

6. Your Data Rights

You have the right to access and delete all your data within the app. If you wish to delete your entire account and all associated Firestore data, you may do so at any time by contacting us at [1019atd@gmail.com]

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 16.
